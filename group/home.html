Welcome to the World of Atari

Founded in 1972 by Nolan Bushnell and Ted Dabney, Atari is widely regarded as the pioneer of the video game industry. It forever changed the entertainment landscape with the release of Pong, one of the first arcade games to gain mainstream popularity.

Atari not only introduced classic games like Asteroids, Centipede, and Missile Command, but also inspired the modern gaming industry by establishing standards in hardware and software development. Its contributions to gaming history are unparalleled.

Featured Games

Pac-Man

Navigate through mazes and avoid ghosts in this iconic arcade game. Pac-Man became one of the most popular games of all time, known for its simple yet addictive gameplay.

Space Invaders

Defend Earth from waves of invading aliens. Space Invaders defined the space shooter genre and is one of the first to use a scrolling screen for gameplay.

Asteroids

Destroy asteroids while avoiding collisions in this classic arcade game. Known for its vector graphics, it pushed the boundaries of what was possible in early video games.
